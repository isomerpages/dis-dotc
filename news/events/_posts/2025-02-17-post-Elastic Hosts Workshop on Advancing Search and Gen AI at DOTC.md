<p>Elastic recently held an interactive and insightful workshop, <strong>"Revolutionise Your Search and Gen AI Strategy,"</strong> at
the DOTC office, bringing together professionals eager to enhance their
search capabilities with cutting-edge AI technologies. Designed as an intermediate-level
session, the workshop provided hands-on learning and expert insights into
optimising search performance and efficiency with Generative AI (Gen AI).</p>

<h4><strong>Unlocking the Power of Search and Gen AI</strong></h4>
<p>Participants gained valuable knowledge on how to evolve their search capabilities
and implement AI-driven enhancements, with key topics including:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Search Maturity Framework</strong> – A roadmap to progress from
basic to advanced search capabilities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Efficiency Unleashed</strong> – Techniques for optimising vector
operations to maximise impact.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Advanced Search Techniques</strong> – Insights into fine-tuning,
metadata optimisation, and lessons learned from ASEAN-based implementations.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The workshop emphasised real-world applications, demonstrating how AI-driven
search can significantly improve accuracy, relevance, and cost-effectiveness.</p>
<h4><strong>Industry Experts Share Their Insights</strong></h4>
<p>The session featured seasoned professionals from Elastic, who provided
expert guidance on building scalable, intelligent search solutions:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Zing Zai, Senior Solutions Architect, Elastic</strong> – With experience
at Elastic and Docusign, Zing specializes in designing scalable architectures
for government and enterprise applications.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Han Xiang, Senior Customer Architect, Elastic</strong> – A Gen
AI practitioner with seven years of experience, Han shared his expertise
in developing applied ML solutions and building AI-powered applications.</p>

<h4><strong>Empowering Participants to Transform Search Experiences</strong></h4>
<p>The workshop attracted professionals seeking to refine their AI-driven
search capabilities, particularly developers working on Gen AI applications.
Attendees walked away with actionable strategies to enhance search relevance,
improve efficiency, and navigate the complexities of modern AI-powered
search systems.</p>
<p>By hosting this workshop at the DOTC office, Elastic provided a collaborative
environment for learning and innovation, reinforcing its commitment to
empowering organisations with the tools and knowledge needed to drive the
future of AI-powered search.</p>
